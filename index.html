Can I work in the UK after graduating?

Unfortunately, it’s not easy for us to stay in the UK


Though the UK government has established the graduate visa, from 2018-2019 alone, only 25% of non EU international students stayed in the UK.



Most Non-EU students works in England. Whereas Scotland have the lowest proportions compared to the rest. It is much recommended to find job opportunities in England rather than Scotland.
